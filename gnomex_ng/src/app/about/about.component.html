Version 6.0

A LIMS for making and tracking requests for genomic analysis (e.g. Next generation sequencing, Microarrays, Sanger Sequencing, etc.)

Import of externally generated genomic datasets in their native formats

Entry and association of structured annotations for the samples and experimental parameters with the data

Web tools to browse, search, modify, and download data files associated with the aforementioned data

GNomEx was developed by Huntsman Cancer Institute Informatics and designed in conjunction with the Microarray and Genomics Analysis Core Facility and the University of Utah Bioinformatics Core
